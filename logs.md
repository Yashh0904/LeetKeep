## 29-01-2026

### Position properties (keu in modals)
- `fixed` steps out of the document and lives relative to the screen
- `absolute` removed from the flow and positions itself relative to the nearest non-static ancestor
- `relative` same as static, but you can now move it freely relative to where it'd normally be
